2 bags pepperidge farm chessmen cookies
6-8 nanners
2 cups milk
1 5oz. box instant french vanilla pudding
8oz pkg. cream cheese, softened
14oz can sweetened condensed milk (Eagle brand)
12oz container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Line a 13x9 dish with 1 bg cookies, followed by bananas
combine milk and pudding in a separate dish, blend
add cream cheese to mix, and condensed milk, whipped topping
top with more cookies
Enjoy!
